One inbox. Every account.
Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, Zoho — every account unified into a single intelligent view. One search searches all of them. One AI reads all of them. Switch between accounts or view everything at once. No more toggling between tabs, missing emails, or losing context.

Find any email. Ask it like a question.
Type exactly what you're looking for — "Did anyone confirm the delivery date?", "Find emails where payment was discussed", "What did Pieter say about the contract in March?" GPT-4.1 reads every email in full and returns exact matches with quotes and relevance scores.

Open a chain that's been going for three weeks across twelve people. Click once. Get an animated timeline, sentiment arc, every commitment made by every party, and a one-line verdict at the top: what this thread is actually about and what you need to do next.
